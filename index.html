Usage Strategy

  • Use 0.3× models (Haiku, Flash, GPT Mini) for heavy-iteration tasks — code completion, quick fixes, linting.
  • Use 1× models (Sonnet 4.6, GPT-5.2, Gemini) for reasoning, complex refactors, and design questions.
  • Reserve 3× models (Opus, large GPT) for architecture decisions, deep code review, or when a 1× model fails multiple times.
  • Check your current burn rate around day 10 — if it exceeds the ideal daily %, shift to cheaper models for the rest of the month.
  • The last 3 days of the month are "sprint" days — if you're under budget you can spend remaining quota freely.

Model costs are approximate — GitHub may adjust multipliers. Actual quota shown at github.com/settings/copilot. Business plan quota resets on the 1st of each month at 02:00 UTC.
